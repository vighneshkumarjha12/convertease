How to Use Our Web App

Seamlessly convert your images to PDF, WebP, PNG, and JPG formats with just a few simple steps.

1

Convert Image to PDF

1. Upload Images: Drag and drop your images into the upload area or click the "Browse" button to select images from your computer.
2. Arrange Images: Optionally, rearrange the order of your images if needed by dragging them within the upload area.
3. Convert: Click the "Convert to PDF" button to start the conversion process.
4. Download PDF: Once the conversion is complete, download your high-quality, merged PDF document by clicking the "Download" button.

2

Convert Image to WebP

1. Upload Image: Drag and drop your image into the upload area or click the "Browse" button to select an image from your computer.
2. Convert: Click the "Convert to WebP" button to start the conversion process.
3. Download WebP: After the conversion is complete, download the optimized WebP file by clicking the "Download" button.

5

Convert PDF to Word

1. Upload PDF: Drag and drop your PDF document into the upload area or click the "Browse" button to select a PDF file from your computer.
2. Convert: Click the "Convert to Word" button to start the conversion process.
3. Download Word Document: Once the conversion is complete, download the converted Word document by clicking the "Download" button.
