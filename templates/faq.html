Frequently Asked Questions

What file formats can I convert? +

You can convert files to PDF, WebP, PNG, and JPG formats. Just upload your image, and we’ll take care of the rest.

Is the service free? +

Yes! Convertease offers free access to all our conversion features 24/7. No hidden charges!

How long does it take to convert a file? +

The conversion process is fast and typically takes only a few seconds, depending on the file size.

Do I need to create an account to use the service? +

No account is required to use Convertease. Simply upload your file, convert it, and download it without any registration process.

Is my data secure? +

Yes! We take security seriously. Your files are processed securely, and we don’t store any of your uploaded data after conversion.

Can I convert multiple files at once? +

Currently, our service allows for converting one file at a time, but we are working on supporting bulk conversions in the future.

What is the maximum file size I can upload? +

The maximum file size allowed is 50MB for each conversion. Larger files may be rejected.

How can I contact support? +

If you need assistance, you can contact our support team by emailing support@convertease.com.

Are there any limitations on the types of images I can upload? +

You can upload images in various formats, such as JPG, PNG, and WebP. However, images with watermarks or low resolution may not convert well.

Can I convert files using a mobile device? +

Yes! Our website is fully mobile-responsive, and you can easily convert files using any smartphone or tablet.
